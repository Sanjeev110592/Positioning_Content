Usefulness of cats

What a terrible scene I saw that day! Huge flames of fire were coming out of each floor, and black and thick smoke spread all around.

Four fire engines were engaged and the firemen in their uniform were playing the hose on various parts of the building. Then the tall ladders of the fire engine were stretched upwards.

Some firemen climbed up with hoses in their hands.

Some firemen climbed up with hoses in their hands. The continuous flooding brought the fire under control but the building was completely destroyed.

Let any one have a plague of rats and mice, as I once had, and let them be delivered therefrom by cats, as I was, and they will have a lasting and kind regard for them.
